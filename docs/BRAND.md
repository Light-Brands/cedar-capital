# Cedar Capital Brand Guidelines

## Brand Essence

**Mission:** Local Austin home buyers offering fast, fair cash offers.

**Brand Personality:**
- Trustworthy like a bank
- Warm like a local family
- Modern + streamlined

---

---

## Voice & Tone

**Do:**
- Be direct and honest
- Use simple, clear language
- Show empathy for seller situations
- Emphasize speed and ease

**Don't:**
- Use pushy sales language
- Make promises you can't keep
- Use jargon or complex terms
- Sound corporate or impersonal

---

## Key Messages

**For Sellers:**
- "Fast, fair cash offer"
- "Close on your timeline"
- "No repairs, no fees, no hassle"
- "Local Austin buyers who care"

**For Investors:**
- "Quality deal flow"
- "Vetted opportunities"
- "Austin market expertise"
